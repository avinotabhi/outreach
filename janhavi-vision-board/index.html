04 — Brand Voice
She sounds like
  • A friend who happens to be an expert — never a lecturer
  • Warm but precise — every word earns its place
  • Confident without being loud — she doesn't need to shout
  • Storytelling that makes you feel before it teaches
  • Same voice on YouTube and Instagram — no split personality
She never sounds like
  • Dramatic, over-produced, neon — her words, not ours
  • Repetitive basic English teacher — she's done with that
  • Niche academic or overly formal
  • Content without a point of view
  • Someone inconsistent across platforms
05 — Do's & Don'ts
Never
  • ✕Bright rainbow colours — she specifically flagged this
  • ✕Plain stark white backgrounds with no warmth
  • ✕Inconsistent visual style across uploads
  • ✕Overdramatic text animations or neon effects
  • ✕Multiple fonts in one design
Always
  • ✓Warm cream or walnut — never stark white
  • ✓One consistent visual template across all content
  • ✓Face prominent, large, confident in every frame
  • ✓B&W with one warm accent colour for contrast shots
  • ✓Same colour palette across YT and Instagram
