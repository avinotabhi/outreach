The Strategic Layer

Content is not enough.
You need IP that only you can own.

The English and communication space on the internet is crowded. Anyone can post a vocabulary reel. What cannot be replicated is a format so distinctly tied to your personality, your story, and your specific lens on the world that the audience associates it with you and only you. That is what an IP is. And that is what we build first.

Based on our conversation, here is the initial IP framework we are proposing — formats built around the three dimensions of who you are and where your audience is headed.

01
Communication That Gets You Hired
Short and long-form content around interview English, cold emailing, networking, and public speaking — skills Jaanvi has lived through and can teach from real experience. Bridges English with career outcomes.
02
The Jaanvi Lens
Opinionated, personal takes — on career, on life abroad, on things nobody in the English space is saying. This is the format that moves you from "English teacher" to someone people follow for your worldview.
03
Real Stories, Real English
Personal storytelling — your journey from Faridabad to medicine to content to Europe — told through the lens of communication. Stories that teach and connect simultaneously. Nobody can replicate this.

These IPs are not rigid boxes. They evolve as your audience grows and as we see what resonates. But they give every piece of content a home — a reason to exist beyond just filling the upload calendar.

Building What You Own

Turn viewers into
an audience you actually own.

Right now, every follower you have belongs to Instagram or YouTube. The moment either platform changes its algorithm — or restricts your reach — you have no direct line to your audience. An email list changes that.

For every YouTube video, we will create a companion resource — a PDF exercise, a vocabulary sheet, an interview checklist — directly tied to the video. Viewers click to download. They land on a simple page. They give you their email. You own that relationship forever, regardless of what any platform does.

This is the exact system used by English with Lucy to build one of the most engaged creator email lists in the education space. We will build it into your YouTube workflow from day one.
